DHNews initiative — Issue #1

First issue of Digital Harbor's internal newspaper, available in digital and print format at the Cochabamba and La Paz offices. It included prize-based activities (holographic and magnetic stickers) to encourage active reading, and an open call for comments on which stories stood out most.
